Festivals of India

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions, where festivals bring people together in celebration. Explore some of the most famous festivals below.

Diwali – Festival of Lights

Diwali is celebrated with lights, diyas, sweets, and fireworks. It symbolizes the victory of good over evil.

Holi – Festival of Colors

Holi is a joyful celebration of colors, marking the arrival of spring and unity among people.

Onam – Harvest Festival

Onam is the biggest festival in Kerala, celebrated with boat races, floral designs, and a grand feast called Onam Sadhya.
